FAQ & Policies

What is your minimum order?

We have a minimum order requirement of $250. This helps ensure we can maintain our high standards of craftsmanship while keeping our prices fair for both our customers and our artisans.

How long does production take?

Each piece is made to order with a production lead time of 2–3 weeks. Since every item is handcrafted, this allows us the time needed to create your jewelry with care and attention to detail.

What are your payment terms?

We require a 50% deposit upfront to begin crafting your order, with the remaining 50% due when your items are ready to ship. This helps cover material costs and supports our small business.

Do you offer shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. The buyer pays for shipping costs, which are calculated at checkout based on your location and order size. Orders over $250 qualify for free domestic shipping.

What is your return policy?

Due to the custom nature of our pieces, we only accept returns for damaged items, which must be reported within 5 days of delivery. Please inspect your order upon arrival and contact us immediately if there are any issues.

How should I care for my jewelry?

To maintain the beauty of your pieces, we recommend storing them in a dry place, avoiding contact with water, chemicals, and perfumes. Gently clean with a soft cloth as needed. Each piece comes with specific care instructions.
