Our Story

Laura's creations are more than just adornments â€” they're wearable expressions of heritage, strength, and individuality. Inspired by timeless metals and soulful stones, every piece is handcrafted with intention.

Founded in 2015, Laura Jewelry Co. began as a small studio in Portland, Oregon. What started as a passion project has grown into a celebrated brand known for its earthy luxury and poetic edge.

Each jewelry piece is thoughtfully designed and meticulously crafted in our studio, using ethically sourced materials and traditional techniques passed down through generations of artisans.

Laura Bennett

Laura Bennett

Founder & Lead Artisan

With a background in fine arts and metalworking, Laura brings a unique perspective to jewelry design. Her journey began at the age of 15 when she inherited her father's jewelry tools and began experimenting with metals and stones, I grew around my dad making jewelries all my life hence the passion.

After graduating from the Rhode Island School of Design, Laura traveled the world studying traditional jewelry-making techniques from master craftsmen in Italy, India, and Japan. These diverse influences are evident in her work, which blends contemporary aesthetics with time-honored craftsmanship.

When not in the studio, Laura can be found hiking in the Pacific Northwest, collecting rare stones, or teaching jewelry-making workshops to aspiring artisans.
